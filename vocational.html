Vocational Programmes

Skills That Last a Lifetime

At Kiyuni Secondary School, every student learns a practical skill alongside their academic subjects — giving them a competitive edge in life, work, and business.

💻

Computer Studies

Digital Literacy & Office Skills

Our computer studies programme introduces students to the fundamentals of computing, digital literacy, and the use of office productivity tools. Students gain hands-on experience with real computers in our computer laboratory.

This programme equips students with skills essential for virtually every modern workplace and university course.

What students learn:

  • Computer basics & hardware
  • Microsoft Word & Excel
  • Internet & email
  • Data entry & typing
  • Digital safety
⌨️

Coding & Programming

Software Development Skills

Our coding programme is one of the most forward-looking offerings in Mubende District. Students learn the fundamentals of programming and software development — preparing them for careers in technology and the digital economy.

From building simple websites to writing basic programmes, students graduate with skills that open doors to tech careers and entrepreneurship.

What students learn:

  • HTML & CSS basics
  • Introduction to programming
  • Web design fundamentals
  • Problem solving with code
  • Digital entrepreneurship
🧵

Tailoring & Fashion

Garment Making & Design

Our tailoring programme teaches students the art and science of garment making, fashion design, and textile work. Using our dedicated tailoring workshop equipped with sewing machines, students develop a trade they can use immediately after school.

Many of our tailoring graduates have started their own businesses in Mubende and beyond.

What students learn:

  • Sewing machine operation
  • Pattern making & cutting
  • Garment construction
  • Fashion design basics
  • Business & pricing skills
🌱

Agriculture & Farming

Modern Farming & Agribusiness

Uganda's economy is deeply rooted in agriculture, and our farming programme prepares students to participate in and benefit from this sector. Using our school farm, students learn modern farming techniques, crop management, and basic agribusiness skills.

This programme links classroom agriculture theory with real practical farming experience on school grounds.

What students learn:

  • Crop production & soil science
  • Animal husbandry basics
  • Modern farming methods
  • Agribusiness management
  • Post-harvest handling
