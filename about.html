Our School

Who We Are

Kiyuni Secondary School is a reputable institution of learning located in Kiyuni Town, Mubende District, Uganda. We are committed to offering quality secondary education at both Ordinary and Advanced levels, guided by the Uganda National Curriculum under the Uganda National Examinations Board (UNEB).

Our school was founded on the belief that every young person in Mubende and the surrounding communities deserves access to quality education that prepares them not only for national examinations, but for life, university, and the world of work.

Beyond academics, we equip our students with practical vocational skills in computer studies, coding, tailoring, and agriculture — ensuring every graduate has both knowledge and a trade.

🎓 O & A Level

Full secondary education from S1 to S6

Our Foundation

Mission, Vision & Motto

🎯

Our Mission

To provide quality, inclusive, and holistic secondary education that equips students with academic excellence, practical skills, and strong moral values to serve Uganda and the world.

🔭

Our Vision

To be a centre of excellence in secondary education in Mubende District, producing well-rounded graduates who are academically competent, skilled, and morally upright.

Head Teacher's Message

A Word From Our Head Teacher

"Education is not merely the passing of examinations — it is the total development of a young person into a responsible, skilled, and confident citizen ready to contribute to Uganda and the world."

Welcome to Kiyuni Secondary School. It is our firm belief that every child who walks through our gates deserves a quality education that prepares them not only for university, but for life itself.

At Kiyuni Secondary School, we work tirelessly to ensure that our students receive the best possible O and A Level education, delivered by dedicated and experienced teachers who genuinely care about the success of every learner.

We are proud of the additional vocational programmes we have put in place — computer studies, coding, tailoring, and agriculture — which give our graduates a competitive edge in both employment and entrepreneurship.

To our students: work hard, stay disciplined, and always remember our motto — Education Determines The Future. Your future is in your hands, and Kiyuni Secondary School is here to guide you every step of the way.

To parents and guardians: thank you for trusting us with your children. We shall not let you down.

Mr. Musa Bosa MpunguHead Teacher, Kiyuni Secondary School
