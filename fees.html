✅ What Fees Cover

  • All classroom teaching and learning
  • Library access and study materials
  • Computer lab and ICT sessions
  • Vocational skills training
  • Sports and co-curricular activities
  • School maintenance and utilities
  • End-of-term examinations and marking

📋 Payment Information

  • Fees are paid per term at the start of each term
  • Payment is made directly to the school bursar
  • A receipt is issued for every payment made
  • Fees must be paid before or at the time of reporting
  • Installment arrangements available — contact bursar
  • UNEB fees are paid separately when applicable
  • Contact school for any fee-related hardships
