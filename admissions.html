Admissions

Join the Kiyuni Secondary School family — applications are open

Home›Admissions
Who Can Join

Open to All Qualified Students

📗

Senior 1 (S1) Entry

For students who have completed Primary 7 (P7) and have their PLE results. This is our main entry point for O Level education.

📘

Senior 5 (S5) Entry

For students who have completed O Level (S4) and have their UCE results. Entry into our A Level programme (Sciences, Arts, or Business combinations).

🔄

Transfer Students

Students transferring from other schools at any level (S2, S3, S4, S6) are welcome. Placement is based on previous results and available space.

How to Apply

Application Process

  1. 1

    Contact the School

    Call us on 0704488636 or 0786512025, or come in person to our offices in Kiyuni Town to express interest and get initial guidance.

  2. 2

    Visit the School

    Come to Kiyuni Secondary School with your child and all relevant academic documents for a guided tour and meeting with the head teacher.

  3. 3

    Submit Documents

    Hand in all required documents at the school office. The admissions team will review and confirm acceptance within a short period.

  4. 4

    Pay School Fees

    Upon acceptance, pay the required fees for the term. A payment receipt will be issued and your child will be officially enrolled.

  5. 5

    Report & Begin School

    Your child reports on the official opening date with all required items. They will be assigned a class, given a timetable, and begin their education at Kiyuni.

📋 Required Documents

  • PLE results slip (for S1 entry) or UCE results (for S5 entry)
  • Previous school's end-of-year/term report
  • Birth certificate or baptism card
  • 4 recent passport-size photographs
  • Transfer letter (for transfer students)
  • Parent or guardian National ID copy
