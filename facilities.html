Our Facilities

Equipped for Excellence

Kiyuni Secondary School provides a safe, well-equipped learning environment that supports both academic and vocational growth.

📚
Academic

Library

A well-stocked library with reference books, textbooks, past papers, and study materials supporting all subjects offered at the school. Students also access our digital library online.

🖥️
Technology

Computer Laboratory

A dedicated computer lab equipped with desktop computers for ICT lessons and vocational coding classes. Students gain real hands-on experience with modern technology.

🏫
Academic

Classrooms

Spacious, well-ventilated classrooms designed for effective learning across all O and A Level subjects. Each room is equipped with teaching aids and furniture for all students.

Environment

School Compound

Our spacious and well-maintained compound provides a clean, safe, and welcoming environment for all students, staff, and visitors to Kiyuni Secondary School.

🧵
Vocational

Tailoring Workshop

A dedicated tailoring room equipped with sewing machines, cutting tables, and materials where students learn garment making, pattern cutting, and fashion design.

🌾
Vocational

School Farm

Practical farming grounds where students learn modern agriculture, crop growing, soil management, and agribusiness skills under real conditions on the school premises.

⚽
Sports

Sports Grounds

Open grounds for football, athletics, netball, and other sports and co-curricular activities, supporting holistic student development beyond the classroom.

🍽️
Welfare

Dining & Canteen

A school canteen where students can access meals and refreshments during break and lunch periods, ensuring students are well-nourished throughout the school day.

🏥
Welfare

Sick Bay

A school sick bay for first aid and basic medical attention to students who fall ill during school hours, with referrals to nearby health facilities when needed.
