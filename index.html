About Our School

A School Built on Excellence, Discipline & Values

Kiyuni Secondary School is a reputable institution located in the heart of Kiyuni Town, Mubende District. We are committed to providing quality secondary education that prepares students for both national examinations and real life.

Under the leadership of our Head Teacher, Mr.BBOSA MUSA MPUNGU, we nurture academic excellence alongside practical vocational skills — ensuring every graduate is confident, skilled and ready for the world.
