About Our School

A School Built on Excellence & Values

Mubende, Uganda

Kiyuni Secondary School is a dedicated institution located in the heart of Kiyuni Town, Mubende District. We offer both O Level and A Level education, preparing our students for national examinations and beyond.

Our mission goes beyond classroom learning. We equip students with vocational and practical skills — from computer technology and coding to tailoring and modern farming — ensuring every graduate is ready for both university and the world of work.

Under the leadership of our Head Teacher, Mr. Musa Bosa Mpungu, Kiyuni Secondary School is committed to nurturing academic excellence, discipline, and community values.

Our Academic Programmes

Comprehensive O and A Level education following the Uganda National Curriculum under UNEB

📗

O Level (S1 – S4)

A broad-based secondary education covering sciences, humanities, languages, and technical subjects leading to the Uganda Certificate of Education (UCE).

Mathematics English Physics Biology Chemistry History Geography
📘

A Level (S5 – S6)

Advanced level studies tailored to university entry requirements, with subject combinations in Sciences, Arts, and Business streams leading to the Uganda Advanced Certificate of Education (UACE).

Sciences Arts Business Subsidiary Maths General Paper

Head Teacher's Message

A Word From Our Head Teacher

"Education is not merely the passing of examinations — it is the total development of a young person into a responsible, skilled, and confident citizen ready to contribute to Uganda and the world."

Welcome to Kiyuni Secondary School. It is our firm belief that every child who walks through our gates deserves a quality education that prepares them not only for university, but for life itself.

We are proud to offer both O and A Level education, supplemented by vocational skills in computing, coding, tailoring, and agriculture. This combination makes our graduates among the most well-rounded young people in Mubende District.

I invite parents and students to be part of the Kiyuni family — where education truly determines the future.

Admissions

How to Join Kiyuni Secondary School

Applications are open. Follow these steps to secure a place for your child.

Application Process

  1. 1

    Contact the School

    Call us on 0704488636 or 0786512025 to express interest and get initial guidance on requirements.

  2. 2

    Visit the School

    Come to Kiyuni Town, Mubende and visit the school with your child and relevant academic documents.

  3. 3

    Submit Documents

    Provide previous school reports, PLE/UCE results (where applicable), birth certificate, and passport photos.

  4. 4

    Pay Fees & Enrol

    Upon acceptance, pay the required fees and your child officially becomes a student of Kiyuni Secondary School.
