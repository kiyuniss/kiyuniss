O Level — Senior 1 to Senior 4

Ordinary Level Education

Our O Level programme runs from Senior 1 (S1) through Senior 4 (S4) and follows the Uganda National Curriculum as set by the Uganda National Examinations Board (UNEB). Students sit the Uganda Certificate of Education (UCE) examinations at the end of S4.

We offer a broad range of subjects covering sciences, humanities, languages, technical, and vocational options, giving every student the opportunity to pursue their strengths while meeting national requirements.

Subjects Offered at O Level:

MathematicsEnglish LanguagePhysicsChemistryBiologyHistoryGeographyLiterature in EnglishLugandaAgricultureCREIREComputer StudiesMusicPhysical EducationFine ArtCommerceEntrepreneurship

A Level — Senior 5 to Senior 6

Advanced Level Education

Our A Level programme covers Senior 5 (S5) and Senior 6 (S6), leading to the Uganda Advanced Certificate of Education (UACE) examinations. A Level is designed for students who have completed O Level and wish to pursue university education.

Students choose subject combinations aligned to their intended university courses. We offer Science, Arts, and Business combinations to cater to every student's career path.

Subjects Offered at A Level:

PhysicsChemistryBiologyMathematicsSubsidiary MathematicsEconomicsHistoryGeographyLiterature in EnglishGeneral PaperEntrepreneurshipICTArt & Design

Examinations

National Examinations

📋

Uganda Certificate of Education (UCE)

Sat at the end of S4. Set and marked by UNEB. Results determine A Level entry and vocational pathways.

🎓

Uganda Advanced Certificate (UACE)

Sat at the end of S6. Results are used for university admissions to Makerere and other institutions.

📊

Internal Assessments

Term examinations, continuous assessment tests (CATs), and mock examinations are conducted every term to track student progress.
